Hands-On Lab

In this section, you will have the opportunity to practice building your own language agents. We have provided you with a number of interactive exercises that will help you learn the basics of language agent design and training.

To get started, select one of the exercises from the list below.

  • Exercise 1: Building a Simple Chatbot
  • Exercise 2: Training a Language Translation Model
  • Exercise 3: Building a Natural Language Processing Model

Once you have completed an exercise, you can submit your code for feedback. We will review your code and provide you with feedback on your design and implementation.
